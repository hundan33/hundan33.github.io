# No-Go Rules

Avoid these even if they appear profitable.

- Anything that requires fake reviews, fake testimonials, or fake accounts.
- Anything that violates platform terms.
- Anything that depends on spam.
- Anything using copyrighted content without permission.
- Anything requiring buyers to believe income is guaranteed.
- Anything requiring local delivery, shipping, or real-world errands.
- Anything requiring private keys in a web page.
- Anything where fees are unknown.
- Anything where a pending transfer can erase the entire spread.
- Anything where customer support burden is larger than the possible profit.
- Anything involving legal, medical, tax, or investment claims you cannot support.
- Anything where you cannot explain the value in one sentence.

If a deal only works when a cost is ignored, it does not work.
