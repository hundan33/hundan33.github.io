Funding-rate watch for fee-recovery checks

Funding rates can look attractive before entry and exit fees are counted. The watch script checks public funding endpoints and estimates how many intervals are needed to cover a simple fee burden.

Latest included scan

42 assets scanned, 120 usable funding rows, 0 rows met the annualized-rate and fee-recovery filters. The strongest observed row was BNB on Binance Futures at about 11.93% annualized, but it needed about 37 funding intervals to cover estimated fees.

Example Command

.\scan-funding-rates.ps1 -Proxy http://127.0.0.1:10808 -TradeUsd 100 -FeePercentPerSide 0.1 -MinAnnualizedPct 20 -MaxIntervalsToCoverFees 6 -ExportCsv latest-funding-watch.csv -ReportPath latest-funding-watch.md

What It Does Not Prove

  • No liquidation risk calculation.
  • No borrow-cost or margin requirement validation.
  • No guarantee the spot and perpetual positions can be opened or closed at shown prices.
  • No tax, exchange, or account-limit review.
