# Deal Evaluation Checklist

Use this before acting on any spread.

## Rights And Rules

- I have the right to use, transform, bundle, or resell the asset.
- The platform terms allow this activity.
- No buyer is being misled.
- No fake review, fake scarcity, or fake income claim is used.
- Any affiliate or sponsored relationship is disclosed where required.

## Numbers

- Buy/acquisition cost is known.
- Sell price is realistic.
- Platform fee is included.
- Payment fee is included.
- Fixed fee per order is included.
- Withdrawal/network/transfer fee is included.
- Refund/support reserve is included.
- Net profit is positive after all fees.
- ROI is high enough to justify the risk.

## Delivery

- Delivery can happen online.
- No local meetup, shipping, or real-world errand is required.
- No custom fulfillment is required after purchase.
- Buyer can access the output without waiting for me.

## Risk

- I understand the worst-case loss.
- I can stop quickly if it does not work.
- I am not using borrowed money.
- I am not using private keys or account credentials in a tool I do not control.
- I am not relying on a price that may disappear before execution.

## Decision

Proceed only if:

- Legal/rules risk is low.
- Net profit remains positive after fees.
- Execution is simple.
- Distribution path exists.
- Worst-case loss is acceptable.
