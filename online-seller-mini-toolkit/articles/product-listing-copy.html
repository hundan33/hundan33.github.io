Product listing copy formula

A good listing tells the buyer what it is, who it is for, what it includes, and what expectations are realistic.

Title Formula

Product type + target buyer + main result Example: Digital Download: Customer Reply Templates for Online Sellers

Description Formula

  • Say who it is for.
  • Name the problem it solves.
  • List exactly what is included.
  • State what it does not guarantee.

This kit is for online sellers who need faster buyer replies. It gives you ready-to-edit messages for common customer situations so you do not start from a blank page. It includes English templates, usage notes, and a simple offline tool. It does not guarantee sales, reviews, or platform outcomes.
