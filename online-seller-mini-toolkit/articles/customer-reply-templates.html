Customer reply templates for online sellers

When buyer messages pile up, the goal is not to sound fancy. The goal is to respond clearly, avoid overpromising, and move the conversation forward.

1. Product Question

Hi, thanks for your interest. This item includes ___. The size/color/version is ___. If you want, I can also send an extra photo before you order.

2. Tracking Delay

Tracking can take 24-48 hours to update after the carrier receives the package. If it still does not update after that, I will help check the status.

3. Damaged Item

I am sorry the item arrived damaged. Please send clear photos of the item, packaging, and shipping label. Once I review them, I will help with the next step.

What To Avoid

  • Do not promise refunds before checking your policy.
  • Do not guarantee carrier delivery dates you do not control.
  • Do not ask for reviews in a way that violates platform rules.
  • Do not argue with angry buyers; ask for evidence and move to the next step.
