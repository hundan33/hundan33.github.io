Simple invoice template for sellers and freelancers

A simple invoice draft should show who is billing, who is paying, what was sold, the total, and how to pay.

Basic Invoice Structure

INVOICE Number: INV-001 Date: 2026-07-02 Due date: 2026-07-02 From: Your Business To: Client Name Items: - Digital product bundle: 1 x $39.00 = $39.00 Subtotal: $39.00 Discount: $0.00 Tax: $0.00 Total: $39.00 Payment: PayPal, Stripe, bank transfer, or agreed payment method Terms: Payment due on receipt. Thank you.

Payment Message

Hi Client Name, invoice INV-001 is ready for $39.00. Payment can be sent by PayPal, Stripe, bank transfer, or agreed payment method. Payment due on receipt. Thank you.

Important

This is a simple drafting structure, not accounting, tax, or legal advice. Some countries and regions require specific invoice fields.
