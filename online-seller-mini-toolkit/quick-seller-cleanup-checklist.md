## Listing

- The title names the product type and main outcome.
- The first sentence explains what the buyer gets.
- The description lists included files or components.
- The description says what is not included.
- The buyer can understand whether it is digital, physical, or service-based.

## Images

- The first image shows the actual product, output, or file preview.
- Text on images is readable on mobile.
- There is at least one image showing what is inside.
- Screenshots do not expose private buyer or order information.

## Delivery

- Digital files are zipped and named clearly.
- The README explains how to open the files.
- HTML tools are tested in a browser.
- Markdown or text templates open in a normal text editor.

## Buyer Messages

- You have a reply ready for product questions.
- You have a reply ready for download issues.
- You have a reply ready for refund requests.
- You have a reply ready for custom work boundaries.

## Payment And Policy

- Price is easy to find.
- Refund policy is simple and platform-compliant.
- No guaranteed-income claim is made.
- No fake scarcity or fake review language is used.
